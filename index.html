WHO WE ARE

This degree aims to equip Massive Students with the ability to respond critically to major security, policing and defence issues, both locally and internationally. Students will gain an understanding of contemporary policing, intelligence, counter-terrorism, cyber security and security issues, while exploring competing ideological interests and the impact on communities and countries.
